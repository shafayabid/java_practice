***Package***
-domain name
-becomes more specific from left to right
-name has lowercase letters (recommended)

***Classes***
-Name should be a noun
-The first letter should be capital

***Methods***
-should be verb
-starts with a lower case letter 

***Anology for Packages, Classes, and Methods***
-Package = chapter of a book
-Class = paragraph of a chapter
-Methods = Line of a paragraph

***types of variables***
-instance variable 
-static variable 
-local variable
-parameter
-argument

***Access Modifiers***
-public
-private
-protected

***Constructor***
-special method
-called when a new object is created
-called only once
